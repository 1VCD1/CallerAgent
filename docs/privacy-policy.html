CallerAgent Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

CallerAgent ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the CallerAgent mobile application.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address.
  • Call data: We store records of calls you initiate, including the company name, phone number, goal, and call transcripts.
  • Device token: We collect your device's push notification token to notify you when a live representative is reached.
  • Personal information: Information you optionally provide in your profile (callback phone number, date of birth) used to assist the AI during calls.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and provide access to the service.
  • To navigate automated phone systems (IVR) on your behalf.
  • To send you push notifications about your active calls.
  • To improve the AI's ability to navigate phone systems over time.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required to operate the service:

  • Twilio: Used to place phone calls on your behalf.
  • Deepgram: Used for real-time speech transcription during calls.
  • OpenAI: Used to power AI decision-making during calls.
  • Firebase (Google): Used for authentication and push notifications.

Data Retention

Call records and transcripts are retained to improve service quality. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communication between the app and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS.

Children's Privacy

CallerAgent is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
